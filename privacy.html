Privacy Policy – lucra

Effective Date: May 25, 2026

lucra (hereinafter referred to as "the App") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of its users in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable data protection laws. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information the App processes, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Scope of Application

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of lucra. By using the App, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described herein.

2. Data Collection and Usage

lucra does not transmit any data to external servers or third parties. All information processed by the App remains exclusively on your device. The App does not require registration or a user account. Specifically:

  • No data is sent to external servers, cloud services, or third parties.
  • No location data, contacts, or device identifiers are collected or tracked.
  • The App does not employ third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.
  • During initial setup, the App optionally asks for your first name for personalization purposes. This name is stored solely on your device and never transmitted.

3. Local Storage of Information

The App stores the following information locally on your device to support its core functionality:

  • Your first name (optional, entered during setup, used for in-app personalization only)
  • Monthly budget, hourly rate, and currency settings
  • Expense entries (amount, category, date)
  • Custom expense categories
  • Saving goals (name, target amount, progress)
  • Recurring expenses (name, amount, frequency)
  • Monthly budget history
  • App settings and preferences (language, display options)

All data remains exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to any server or third-party service. Uninstalling the App will permanently remove all locally stored data.

4. Local Notifications

lucra may send local notifications to your device to support your financial goals. These include:

  • Weekly spending summaries (every Sunday)
  • Monthly review reminders (end of each month)
  • Budget alerts when spending reaches 80% or 100% of your monthly budget
  • Saving goal completion alerts

These notifications are generated entirely on your device and are not processed or transmitted by any external server. You can disable notifications at any time in your device's Settings.

5. Siri and App Intents

lucra supports Siri integration via Apple's App Intents framework. This allows you to log expenses and savings using voice commands. Any data processed through Siri is handled in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy. lucra itself does not transmit any data resulting from Siri interactions to external servers.

6. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the GDPR, lucra's handling of local data is based on legitimate interest for the operation and enhancement of the App. No personal data is processed for advertising, marketing, or profiling purposes.

7. User Rights

Since lucra does not process personal data on external systems, the rights provided under GDPR (including access, correction, deletion, restriction, and data portability) are largely inapplicable. All data can be permanently deleted by uninstalling the App. Users may contact the developer for any privacy-related inquiries.

8. Data Security

All locally stored data is protected by the security mechanisms of the iOS operating system. No external transmission occurs, minimizing exposure to unauthorized access. Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their devices.

9. Data Sharing and Third Parties

lucra does not sell, rent, or trade any user data. No personal information is shared with third parties, except when legally required by authorities or in cases to protect the rights of the App or its users.

10. Children's Privacy

lucra does not knowingly collect or process personal data from children. Since no data is transmitted externally, the App is safe for use by minors under the supervision of a parent or guardian.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in the App's functionality or legal requirements. The current version will always be available on this page. Continued use of lucra constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

12. Contact Information
